What is MCP-Server?

The Model Context Protocol (MCP) Server is a powerful backend that allows you to connect and manage various AI models from different providers (like Ollama, Groq, or OpenAI). It gives you a central hub to configure, deploy, and interact with specialized AI assistants tailored to your needs.

Why Mimi?

Mimi is the friendly user-facing persona of the assistants you create. Whether it's managing your schedule, providing information, or acting as the central nervous system for your IoT devices, Mimi is built to be helpful, context-aware, and seamlessly integrated into your daily tasks.
